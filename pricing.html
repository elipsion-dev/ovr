How We Price

Priced by Volume

Our removal fee is based on the volume of items you have — what we call "the load of the truck." You only pay for how much of the truck you fill up.

Our truck dimensions: 14 ft long × 8 ft wide × 5 ft tall capacity. The minimum charge is ¼ of a truckload.

All-Inclusive Service

All you do is point — we carry, load, and haul away everything. We broom sweep when finished. Price quoted is price paid.

What We Don't Accept:
  • Tires
  • Paint cans
  • Hazardous materials

How We Determine Your Price

Priced by Truck Volume

Your price is based on how much of our truck your items will fill. Text us photos for a quote — usually answered within minutes.

¼ Truckload
Minimum load

A few items — a couch and loveseat, a few appliances, or a small bedroom cleanout. This is the minimum load for all jobs.

  • 1–3 large items (sofa, dresser, appliance)
  • Several smaller items / boxes
  • Small room cleanout
½ Truckload

A medium-sized load — typical garage cleanout, a full bedroom set, or a mix of furniture and misc. items.

  • Several large furniture pieces
  • Multiple appliances
  • Half-garage cleanout
¾ Truckload

Our most common load size — estate cleanouts, full garage cleanouts, rental property turnovers, large declutters.

  • Full garage or basement cleanout
  • Multi-room estate contents
  • Storage unit (medium to large)
Full Truckload

Maximum capacity — whole-home cleanouts, warehouse cleanouts, complete property clearances.

  • Whole-home or full estate cleanout
  • Warehouse or commercial space
  • Full property clearance
